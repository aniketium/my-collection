About Inventory

Inventory is a platform for people who want to be more mindful about what they own. We believe the objects we surround ourselves with matter — they shape our daily experience, productivity, and well-being.

Create your personal catalog of everything you own. See the full picture: how many things you have, their total value, and what categories dominate your life. Every item tells a story about a choice you made.

Share your collection with others. Discover what people you admire own and recommend. And when something no longer serves you, list it for sale in our community marketplace.
